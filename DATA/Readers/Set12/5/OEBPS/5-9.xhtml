"Giving Razz sweets is about the worst way

to make him happy," said Miss Vie. "Razz is

too fat and lazy to have any fun. Max, if you

keep feeding Razz like that, you may just

love him to death. He will become another

victim of the Confection Connection." Max felt

his throat get tight and dry. He looked down

at the floor. "Oh," was all that he could say.
