Pete's father was chosen to help coach the

team. He knew it would be an unusually

hard season for the Eagles. The other

teams were very good. He wanted to help

this new team play together.

Pete's dad was a good coach.

He kept them busy at soccer practice.

They had to kick the ball over, under, above,

and through a pretty hard course. He taught

them that lots of different people with

different skills can pull together as a team.
